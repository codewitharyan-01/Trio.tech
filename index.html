What We
Won't Build.
Scam or Misleading Sites
We don't build anything designed to deceive users. Every website we ship must represent real value to real people honestly.
Competitor Copy Projects
If your brief is "make me exactly like [competitor]" — we're not the right fit. We build originals that outperform, not replicas.
No-Business-Behind-It
If there's no real product, real service, or real team behind the site, we won't build it. We build for substance, not appearance.
Predatory Dark Patterns
Fake countdown timers, hidden charges, deceptive UX — none of it. We design for trust, not manipulation.
Rushed Without Process
We don't take projects where there's no time to do it right. A rushed website hurts your brand more than no website.
Anything Unethical
Our work is our reputation. If we wouldn't be proud to show it to our next client, we won't build it for you.
